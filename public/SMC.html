Assassin's Creed Odyssey, an action game developed by ubique Quebec studios and distributed by ubisoft, is the 11th master series of Assassin's Creed and a sequel to the 2017 game Assassin's Creed: origins. Set in 431 BC, four centuries before the assassin's creed: origins, the game tells the secret history of the peloponnesian war between ancient Greek city-states. Players play as a mercenary and can join the tirol alliance led by Athens or the peloponnesian league led by Sparta. The game will be released on October 5, 2018, and will be available on PC, PS4, Xbox One, nintendo Switch and other platforms, where the nintendo Switch version will run as a cloud game. The assassin's creed: Odyssey takes place in ancient Greece, a world of mystery and legend, during the famous peloponnesian war between Sparta and Athens in the 5th century BC. Players will choose to play "Alexis" or "Cassandra," a Spartan mercenary condemned to death by the family, embarking on an epic journey from a young abandoned man to a legendary hero, and gradually uncover the truth behind his mysterious lineage.
