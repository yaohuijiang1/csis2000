The last PUBG is a tactical competitive shooting sandbox game. In this game, players need to collect various resources on the game map and fight other players in the shrinking security area to survive to the end. In addition to winning the top g-star award for President and five other awards, the game "last jedi" broke seven guinness world records. On August 9, 2018, "" survival of the jedi" "officially announced that it will start" "100 days of action" "to conduct self-examination campaigns lasting several months, providing players with a better game experience The island is called Erangel, and in the game, Erangel is located in Crimea A small island that was captured by the Soviet union after world war ii, the soviets erased the island from all maps and turned it into a secret research base, as well as conducting biological and chemical experiments on the locals. The island was abandoned when a group of rebels bombed several biological institutes, causing everyone to evacuate the island. Each game involves 100 players parachuting into the corners of the map, searching for weapons, vehicles and supplies, and fighting across a wide variety of terrain. To win, strategy is as important as marksmanship. During the game, there will be a blue circle to deport players to a smaller place to exchange fire. The blue circle is commonly known as the "electric circle" or "poison circle" -- the term comes from the reduced chlorine sphere in the same type of game H1Z1, which the developers say is some kind of electric field released by some kind of electric transmitter that was supposed to suppress the resistance of the Erangel locals. Each game of the game will be randomly converted to a safe area, and each area received weapons, props are random.
